Website functionality.

The website has a few key functionalities, but I will name them all so that you as the reader has a clear understanding of the code behind each part of the website. To start off we'll start with the home page, which has all the information about the different types of wrist watches, on that page there are only two functions; the hyperlinks that send you to the respective official home page of each wrist watch. The home page also consists of the 'Explore' dropdown-content button. The hyperlinks, you'll see them in 'action' when you click any watch under the 'The types of wrist watches' part of the webpage. For visual aid for the reader, when you 'hover' the cursor over any watch, the background-color changes to a darker shade so that you have a visual understanding of which watch you currently 'hovering' over. The button contains a dropdown list with 'About' and 'Group Members' options. Like the hyperlinks when you click on the 'Explore' button and 'hover' over any of the two options in the dropdown-content, the background-color changes into a darker shade so that you know what content you're currently hovering over and about to click. When you click on any of the two dropdown-content they will send you to that respective content page, and that is where you will see the necessary information. In the 'About' page there are two functionalities, and they are the link that sends you back to the home page when you click on it and the hyperlink that sends you the the home page of Forbes. The only functionality on the 'Group Members' page is the 'Go back to home page' hyperlink, which takes you back to the Home page. Lastly to add, even though its not a functionality the website contains a 'favicon' in the title to help give the reader an understanding that the following website is about watches.
